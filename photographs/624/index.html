Waterfall Nude

  • Digital scan from film negative
  • Photographed October 2025
  • Sonora Pass, California
  • 8x10" film
  • N+2 development
  • 12" lens
  • no filter
  • f/22
  • Not available as a print
Video

I've never photographed nudes, but it felt right to approach this waterfall scene with a boudoir sensibility. The texture contrast between rock and water feels lacy and intimate. I'd normally chalk this up to going just a bit nutty after extended time alone, but this was day one of my most recent photo trip. The first photograph is usually the most difficult but this one arrived easily.
